Projection Of Straight Lines

 Draw a parallel line from XY in VP at 20mm distance(from a') from XY and in HP at 30mm distance(from a) from XY
STEP  5 
 Draw line of 70mm at a' at x° from HP and mark other end as b1'.
STEP  6 
 Similarly Draw line of 70mm at a y° from VP and mark other end as b1.
STEP  7 
 Draw projection line of b1' till it meet the parallel line in HP and mark it b2
STEP  8 
 Similarly, Draw projection line of b1 till it meet the parallel line in VP and mark it b2'.
